Hello! My name is Amber Rodelo and I am a second year at University of California, Riverside. I am currently studying Gender and Sexuality Studies and I am very passionate about this field. I have seen first hand how impactful Social Work can be as my brother, Marcus, was adopted into our family. From the beginning my mother has always been open about her expereince as a foster mom and answered any questions any of my siblings had. She would tell us about the social worker on her case and how she is eternally grateful for what she did for our family. Because without her, we wouldn't be where we are today.
